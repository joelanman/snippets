Rich text editors

We have recognised a need in DfE SDD projects for a rich text editor in place of standard text areas.

AMSD Caseworkers in particular require a rich text option for creating narratives to put forward for review by senior colleagues and ministers.

The Ministry of Justice have a rich text editor component which we originally intended to use. We have since learned that this is being phased out of use due to some accessibility issues, and that they are looking at third-party rich text editors.

We have a pressing need, and so I have created test pages for a number of the most popular rich text editors here.

  • CKEditor
  • CLEditor
  • Quill
  • SCEditor
  • TinyMCE
  • Trumbowyg
